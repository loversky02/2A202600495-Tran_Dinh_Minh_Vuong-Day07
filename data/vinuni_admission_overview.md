# VinUni Admission Overview

## About VinUniversity

VinUniversity (VinUni) is a premier private university in Vietnam, known for its world-class education, modern facilities, and strong industry connections. Partnering with top global institutions like Cornell University and the University of Pennsylvania, VinUni provides an internationally recognized degree.

## Admission Requirements

### Basic Requirements:
1. High school diploma or equivalent certificate (A Level/IB)
2. English Proficiency: IELTS 6.5 or higher, with no skill below 6.0
3. GPA: 8.0 or higher (recommended – grades are just one evaluation factor)
4. AACC Standards (Aspiration, Ability, Academic, Creativity)

### Alternative English Proficiency Tests:
- TOEFL iBT: Minimum 79 with at least 20 in writing, 18 in speaking, 15 in reading, and 15 in listening
- Pearson Test of English (Academic): Minimum total score of 58 with no communication skill below 50
- Cambridge Advanced English (CAE): Minimum 176 with no skill below 169 in any part

**Note:** If you do not meet the English proficiency requirement of IELTS 6.5 (or equivalent), you can take the English Placement Test to be placed in additional English proficiency classes through the Pathway English Program (usually taught in the summer semester).

## Admission Rounds

There are three admission rounds:

1. **Early Round:** October 15, 2024 – January 15, 2025
2. **Regular Round:** February 15, 2025 – May 15, 2025
3. **Rolling Round:** June 15, 2025 – August 15, 2025

**Special Benefit:** Applicants who submit their applications during the Early Round will receive a waiver of the application fee worth 2,000,000 VND if they participate in VinUni Open Day or other admission events.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare your documents
- Personal information
- 1-page CV (VinUni template)
- Academic records: high school transcripts, English proficiency certificate, other academic certificates (if any)
- Short essay
- Extracurricular activities/achievements (Any other achievements, certificates, if applicable)
- Information of referees (teachers who know the applicant best)
